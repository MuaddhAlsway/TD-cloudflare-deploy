# B2B E-commerce Client "The Partnership Seeker"

Khalid bin Hassan

E-commerce Business Owner, Founder of Premium Electronics Online Store

**Age:** 35

**Location:** Jeddah, Saudi Arabia

> "I need a logistics partner who will enhance my brand reputation, not damage it - my customers' delivery experience directly reflects on my business."

## About Khalid

Khalid founded his electronics e-commerce business three years ago and has experienced steady growth, but logistics challenges are becoming his biggest operational headache. He understands that delivery quality directly impacts customer satisfaction and retention. He's actively seeking a logistics partner that can handle his specialized needs including cash-on-delivery, returns processing, and maintain the premium service level his brand promises to customers.

## Behavioral Considerations

- Researches logistics partners thoroughly, reading client testimonials and examining service capabilities before making contact
- Needs detailed information about pricing, service levels, and integration processes before committing to partnerships
- Values transparent communication and professional presentations that reflect well on both businesses
- Expects dedicated account management and business-grade service standards rather than consumer-level support

## Frustrations

- Current logistics providers create customer service problems that damage his brand reputation
- Difficult to find comprehensive information about specialized services like COD handling and reverse logistics
- Generic logistics companies don't understand the unique needs of premium e-commerce businesses
- Lack of social proof and client references makes it hard to evaluate potential partners' credibility

## Goals

- Partner with a logistics provider that enhances rather than compromises his customer experience
- Access comprehensive logistics services (delivery, COD, returns) through a single trusted partner
- Establish long-term business relationships with dedicated account management and support

## Tasks

- Research and evaluate logistics partners' service offerings and client success stories
- Submit detailed quote requests for custom delivery solutions and pricing
- Review client testimonials and social proof to validate partnership decisions
- Access dedicated sales consultation to discuss specialized business requirements
- Onboard as a new client and establish account management relationships
