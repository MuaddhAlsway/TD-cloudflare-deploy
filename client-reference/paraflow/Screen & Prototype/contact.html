Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common support inquiries.

How can I track my shipment?

Use our tracking tool on the homepage by entering your tracking number (TD + 9 digits). You can also track via WhatsApp or call our hotline for real-time updates.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver Sunday through Thursday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Friday deliveries are available in select areas.

How do I report a delivery issue?

Contact us immediately via phone (+966 11 234 5678), WhatsApp, or email. Provide your tracking number and describe the issue for fastest resolution.

Can I reschedule my delivery?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time via WhatsApp, phone, or through our customer portal. No additional charges apply.
